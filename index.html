Testing Information

Know your test dates & options.

Questions about testing? See Mrs. Kathleen Pereira (Assessment Coordinator) or your school counselor. Fee waivers may be available.

📐

SAT (Digital)

Administered at WHS and test centers. Register at collegeboard.org. Ask your counselor about fee waivers.

🧪

ACT

National testing dates throughout the year. Free prep via Khan Academy. Register at act.org.

📗

PSAT / NMSQT

Taken each October at WHS. Juniors in the top 1% qualify for National Merit Scholarship consideration.

📘

AP & AICE Exams

AP: Contact Mr. Lee Tanton. AICE: Contact Ms. Mierka Drucker. Scores of 3+ (AP) or C+ (AICE) may earn college credit.

Florida-Specific Aid

Bright Futures & Florida Scholarships

Florida offers some of the nation's best state scholarship programs. WHS Wolverines — here's what you need to qualify.

⭐

Florida Academic Scholars (FAS)

Covers 100% tuition at FL public colleges. Requires 3.5 GPA, 1290 SAT or 29 ACT, and 100 community service hours.

🌟

Florida Medallion Scholars (FMS)

Covers 75% tuition. Requires 3.0 GPA, 1170 SAT or 26 ACT, and 75 community service hours.

🏛️

Florida State University System

12 public universities including UF, FSU, FAU, UCF, FIU, FGCU, and more. Apply via floridashines.org.

📋

Community Service Hours

Log hours for Bright Futures through your school. Deadline for current seniors: December 1. See your counselor for log sheets.

Bright Futures Portal → Florida Shines →
